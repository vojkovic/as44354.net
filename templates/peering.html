Peering Policy

    AS44354 has an open peering policy, we will peer with any network as long you meet the following basic requirements:

  • You have a publically routable ASN and IPv6 address space.
  • You have a complete and up-to-date PeeringDB profile.
  • You provide a NOC contact email at the time of peering.
  • Peering is done over a physical connection (no virtual peering).
  • Only send us traffic from your own or your downstream networks.
  • You will not announce a full table except by prior agreement.

    • We also reserve the following rights:

  • Change our peering policy at any time without notice.
  • Cease peering at any time without notice.
  • Withdraw routes at any time without notice.

    • If you meet the above requirements and would like to peer with us, please see Contact for more information.
