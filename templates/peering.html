Peering

    AS44354 has an open peering policy, we will peer with any network as long you meet the following basic requirements:

  • You have a publically routable ASN and IPv6 address space.
  • You have a complete and up-to-date PeeringDB profile.
  • You only send us traffic from your own or your downstream networks.

    • We also reserve the following rights:

  • To change our peering policy at any time without notice.
  • To cease peering at any time without notice.
  • To withdraw routes at any time without notice.
